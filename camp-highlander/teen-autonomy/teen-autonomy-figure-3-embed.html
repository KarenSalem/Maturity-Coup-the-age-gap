Teen labor force participation, 1948 to 2024

Values show the annual average labor force participation rate for Americans ages 16–19, as published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics from the Current Population Survey.

Dashed segments cover years before 1963 in the published historical series, which are sampled at decennial intervals. Solid segments cover 1963 to 2024.

Sources: U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, Current Population Survey, annual averages for ages 16–19. Current Population Survey. Chart compiled by Camp Highlander.
