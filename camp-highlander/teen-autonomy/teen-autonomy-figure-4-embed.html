Young adults living in the parental home, by sex, 1960 to 2025

Values show the share of Americans ages 18–24 living in a parental home, by sex. CPS counts unmarried college students living in dorms as living in the parental home.

Dashed segments are decennial Census observations for 1960, 1970, and 1980. Solid segments are annual Current Population Survey ASEC estimates beginning in 1983.

Sources: U.S. Census Bureau, Historical Living Arrangements of Adults, Table AD-1. Historical Living Arrangements of Adults. Chart compiled by Camp Highlander.
