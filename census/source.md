# Census Living Arrangements

- Historical Living Arrangements of Adults: https://www.census.gov/data/tables/time-series/demo/families/adults.html
- AD-1 table: Young Adults, 18-34 Years Old, Living At Home: 1960 to Present
- AD-3 tables: Living Arrangements of Adults 18 and Over, plus age-group cuts for 18-24 and 25-34
- Related figure: AD-1 Young adults living in the parental home
- Source coverage: decennial censuses, 1960-1980; Current Population Survey Annual Social and Economic Supplements, 1983-present
- Fields used: year, sex, age band, living-at-home share, living arrangement category
- Editorial use: household independence / moving out / parental-home dependence
- Current benchmark chart: `census-living-at-home-benchmark.svg` using published 1960 and 2022 figures for 18-24 and 25-34 adults living at home
- Full trend chart: `census-ad1-longtrend.svg` using the AD-1 workbook from 1960 to present
- Local extract: `ad1-living-at-home.csv` with year, age band, sex, and parental-home share
