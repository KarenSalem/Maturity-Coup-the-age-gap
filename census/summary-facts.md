# Census Starter Facts

- 18-24, parental home:
  - 1960: men 52%, women 35%
  - 2022: men 57%, women 55%
- 25-34, parental home:
  - 1960: men 11%, women 7%
  - 2022: men 19%, women 12%
- Quick read: the parental-home share rises sharply for young women and also climbs for young men, especially in the 18-24 band.
- Census counts unmarried college students in dorms as living in the parental home for CPS-based figures.
- Best next extraction target: AD-1, because it gives the longest young-adult home-living series and anchors the full long trend.
