# ATUS Social Connection

- ATUS overview: https://www.bls.gov/tus/overview.htm
- ATUS tables page: https://www.bls.gov/tus/tables.htm
- Socializing chart source: https://www.bls.gov/charts/american-time-use/activity-leisure.htm
- Alone/with others table: https://www.bls.gov/tus/tables/a8-2024.pdf
- Fields used: age band, average hours per day socializing and communicating, average waking hours alone, average waking hours with others present
- Editorial use: social connection / time alone / support layer
- 2020 ATUS annual estimates were not produced because fielding was suspended during the pandemic
- Microdata target: annual 2014-2024 activity-summary files for age-specific socializing and communicating, plus alone/with-others measures when feasible
